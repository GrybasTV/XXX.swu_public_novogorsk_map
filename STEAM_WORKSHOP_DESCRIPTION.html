Ukraine vs Russia: Warmachine

Modified and adapted version of the original Warmachine Conquest mission, specifically tailored for the Ukraine vs Russia 2025 conflict. This mission features custom factions, improved gameplay mechanics, and numerous bug fixes.

Key Modifications from Original

  • Custom Factions: Ukraine 2025 and Russia 2025 factions
  • Custom Loadouts: 19 unique loadouts per faction (WEST 800-818 for Ukraine, EAST 200-218 for Russia)
  • Modern Units: Ukrainian forces feature UA_SSO, UA_TRO, and UA_MV units; Russian forces use RHS MSV units
  • FPV Drones: Support for FPV kamikaze drones for both factions
  • Improved AI Respawn: Configurable AI respawn delays with dynamic scaling based on game progress
  • Bug Fixes: Fixed respawn timers, loadout system, vehicle respawn cycles, and sector task assignments
  • Enhanced Stability: Multiple fixes for undefined variables, syntax errors, and multiplayer synchronization

Original Features

  • Conquest game mode with sector control
  • Dynamic mission generator
  • 1-48 players support (SP/Coop/PvP)
  • Autonomous AI that joins the battle
  • Vehicle respawn system
  • FOB/BASE building
  • Artillery and CAS support
